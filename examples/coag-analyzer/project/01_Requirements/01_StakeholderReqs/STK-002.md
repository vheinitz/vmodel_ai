# Sub-15-minute result turnaround

## Need

The system must deliver a PT (prothrombin time) result for a loaded sample within
15 minutes of sample placement, end to end.

## Context

The lab's STAT workflow caps turnaround at 30 minutes from blood draw to result
report. Pre-analytical handling consumes ~10 minutes, leaving the instrument a
~15-minute budget if the lab's commitments are to be met.

## Acceptance

Median end-to-end time for the first PT result in a batch is below 15 minutes
across a 100-sample acceptance run, measured from sample-load to result-publish.
