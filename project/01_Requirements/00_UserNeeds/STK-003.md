# Electric-Powered Operation

## Need

The device shall be electrically powered and operate from a standard laboratory mains power supply. It must be ready for use without warm-up procedures or calibration cycles at the start of each working day.

## Context

Laboratory instruments are expected to run reliably on mains power available in all modern medical labs. Battery-powered devices introduce concerns about charge state, battery degradation, and inconsistent mixing speeds. The device should plug in and work.

## Acceptance

Laboratory staff will consider this need met when:
- The device connects to a standard wall outlet and powers on immediately
- No preparatory charging, manual cranking, or complex setup is required before use
