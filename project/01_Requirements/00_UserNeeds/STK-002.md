# Compatibility with Standard Laboratory Tubes and Containers

## Need

The device shall accept standard laboratory tubes and small containers commonly used in medical labs (e.g., blood collection tubes, microcentrifuge tubes, reagent vials). It must securely hold containers of various sizes during mixing without adapters that are easily lost or damaged.

## Context

Lab staff handle multiple container types daily. Requiring specialized or proprietary containers would disrupt workflow, increase costs, and reduce adoption. The device should work with what the lab already uses.

## Acceptance

Laboratory staff will consider this need met when:
- Commonly used tube types and small containers fit securely in the device without modification
- Containers remain stable during mixing (no dislodging or tipping)
- No special adapters are needed for standard lab consumables
