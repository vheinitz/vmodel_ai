# Remote serviceability

## Need

Service engineers must be able to retrieve diagnostic logs and instrument
state from a deployed instrument over the network without on-site presence.

## Context

Average on-site visit costs ~€1500. Internal KPI targets reducing field
service calls by 40 %. Remote access must not weaken the instrument's
attack surface: only authenticated service personnel may read diagnostic
data, and the channel must be encrypted.

## Acceptance

A service engineer can retrieve the last 48 hours of diagnostic logs from
a customer instrument over an authenticated, encrypted channel.
