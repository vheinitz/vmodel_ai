# Reproducible analytical performance

## Need

The system must achieve a coefficient of variation (CV) of 5 % or lower on
duplicate measurements of the same sample for normal-range PT.

## Context

IVDR Annex I §9.1 and the lab's internal QC protocol require demonstrated
reproducibility. Above 5 % CV the assay no longer supports the clinical
decisions it is used for.

## Acceptance

Across 20 duplicate measurements of three reference materials (low, normal,
high control), CV per material is below 5 %.
